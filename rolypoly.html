Description

Rolypoly was once Kinetic's wheel and it became sentient after reaching enlightenment during the March 2023 NHRL competition. In Rolypoly's journey, he ate a radioactive fly and became a bug wheel. He sits on the CRC shelf providing wisdom.

Design

Rolypoly is a Banebots wheel and is blue. His radioactive powers gave him antennas that can hear all.

Trivia
