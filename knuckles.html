Knuckles "The Echidna"

Builders

Katie Huntley, Sana Gaya, Shawn Chen.

Description

Knuckles is a dangerous combat robot featuring 2 wedges, 2 spikes, 1 flipper, and 1 flamethrower. Prepare to be scorched, flipped, and stabbed at the hands of this short-tempered bot.

Design

Knuckles is an exceptionally formidable combat robot, boasting an impressive array of offensive and defensive weaponry. This mechanical marvel is equipped with two menacing wedges designed to effectively control the battlefield. Knuckles is armed with two menacing spikes that can pierce through opponents with ease, leaving them in a state of disarray. Its one-of-a-kind flipper adds to its versatility, allowing it to expertly toss adversaries into the air, leaving them vulnerable to a swift defeat. To add to the peril, Knuckles wields a fearsome flamethrower, capable of unleashing torrents of searing flames upon its foes. In the merciless hands of this short-tempered bot, opponents must prepare for a relentless onslaught, as they are in danger of being scorched, flipped, and ruthlessly stabbed into submission.

Trivia
