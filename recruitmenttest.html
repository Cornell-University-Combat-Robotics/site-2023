What Can You Look Forward to With CRC's New Member Experience?

Last year our newbies had a blast getting to work alongside the team with our newbie training program! Here is just a glimpse of what your upcoming time with CRC will look like.

Late October

Meeting the team! We welcome you to CRC by introducing you to the rest of the team and start you off with some basic information to get you started on the team.

Early November

First GBody Meeting: New members get the chance to experience their first GBody Meeting alongside the team, catching up on all the new developments within each subteam while enjoying a variety of memes that members like to put together for these weekly meetings.

Early November

Newbie Training Program: Newbies get a head start on their 3lb Projects, with CRC mentors guiding them through the ideation process while explaining all the various parts that go into creating competitive combat robots.

Early December

3lb Project Showcase! After finalizing your 3lb Projects with the help of CRC mentors, brushing up on CAD designs and creating a presentation to showcase to the team, you and your project teammates will have the opportunity to show off all your hardwork from this past semester creating a 3lb robot from scratch! Family and friends are welcome to join and share pizza with the team.

Mid-December

Competition Day! Watch our robots compete at the Norwalk Havoc Robot League Competition, and through funding and sponsorship CRC strives to cover the costs for all our members to be able to attend competition day.

January

Newbie Graduation! Newbies are officially welcomed as full-fledged members of their respective subteams.

Our 2022 recruitment season has finished. Join us next September for our 2023 recruitment season!

FAQ

Does Combat Robotics at Cornell offer class credits?

Yes! As a project team run through Cornell, you will be able to receive credits for being on the team starting in the spring semester.

Do I need any prior experience?

We take members of all experience levels. When our new members first join CRC, we take them all through our training program to make sure that all members are able to catch up and explore all the different aspects of the team.
