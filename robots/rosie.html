Rosie "The Rizz-ler"

Description

Rosie is a horizontal spinner robot whose weapon barrages the opponent with a hefty hunk of steel. Once an opponent is struck by her weapon, they are knocked across the arena, taking damage from the force of impact of both her weapon and hitting the wall. Rosie's top plate comes from Cornell's very own Rapid Prototyping Lab and not only protects her from hostile attacks but also gives her a great amount of unspoken rizz. Rosie's personality
