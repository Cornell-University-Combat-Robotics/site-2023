Samurai "The Bloodied"

Builders

Vishnu Nair, Javohir Abdurazzakov, Jenny Wu, Manuely Feliz Portes

Description

The Samurai is a battle bot that resembles the mask of a samurai. It has ramps on all sides and two spinners that flips and deals damage to other bots. It is the best bot to ever touch the competition.

Design

The Samurai has both passive and active weapons. The passive weapon is the ramps that the bot is equipped with that works both as defense and offense. It defends us from ramming and other impacts that hit the side of our bot. The ramp would lift and parry the attack. It would also work on offense because it would guide the other robot towards our active weapon, the spinner. The spinner spins really fast at the top of our ramp with the purpose of flipping other bots, dealing damange and knocking them out of balance.
