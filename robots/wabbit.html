Design

Wabbit is a two-wheel-drive vertical spinner with an HDPE chassis. Standing tall on two aluminum weapon uprights (his ears), he uses his wedges to lift opponents into an ultra-sharp weapon. FingerTech foam wheels top off the package for a reliable direct-drive system, so Wabbit can charge quickly and watch as his dented opponents fly through the air.

Trivia

  • Wabbit was one of the first bots ever designed by CRC.
  • Wabbit's battery, ESCs and motors are all constrained, we swear.
  • Wabbit's initial design had wayyy too many screws, so his nickname is Dentist Bot.
  • Wabbit has a fear of holy hand grenades.
