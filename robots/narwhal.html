TRUST Narwhal is a 3lb robot of CRC Class of Fall '23. It was made in real life!! It has a spinning weapon, a sturdy chassis (trust) and a sloped tail (trust) also it is totally within weight limit.

The Narwhal is very deadly armed (it has a spinning rod that is not suspiciously shaped). Its chassis poses no challenge to the 3lb weight limit. it does it best and CRC loves it very much. BUY NAUGHTY NARWAL MERCH.
