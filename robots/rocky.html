Rocky "The Revolutionary"

Builders

Marcus Esposito, Mohammad Ali Moghaddasi.

Description

Rocky is a born fighter, much like his movie-star famous namesake. Rocky was tired of the boring spinners that took over competition and even more bored with the alternative, flippers. After an 80's inspired training montage, Rocky emerged, ready to knock out the competition.

Design

Rocky is a compact, two-wheeled robot with a pneumatic-powered punch. The chassis is made out of lightweight and strong HDPE with an aluminum “fist”. Rocky is stabilized with a caster in the back of his chassis. Rocky's chassis is spacious and easily opens for access to repair and replace any internal components.

Trivia

  • Rocky's original name was Robert Balboa.
  • Rocky's uses a roller to help move around the arena.
  • Rocky is powered with a CO2 bike tire inflator.
  • Rocky's pneumatic pistons are from a Halloween store.
