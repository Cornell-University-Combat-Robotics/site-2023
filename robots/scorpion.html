Description

Introducing Scorpion, the epitome of precision and power in the realm of 3lb battlebots. The Scorpion, widely regarded as the Greatest of All Time, is a 3 lb robot modeled after the fearsome scorpion. It has an impressive track record, boasting 100 wins and 0 losses. It retired with an undefeated record and won 15 major world championships. As an amateur, he won a bronze medal in the featherweight division at the 1996 Olympics, three U.S. Golden Gloves championships (at light flyweight, flyweight, and featherweight), and the U.S. national championship at featherweight.

Power in its tail, victory's sweet collision, Where destruction's the language, and it's Scorpion's vision.
