Builders

Luke Murphy.

Description

Milio is a Tamiya RC car assembled by Luke Murphy. Luke assembled him when he was 11 years old at a summer camp. Luke decided to bring Milo to CRC to use as a sparring bot against Lance since we had no other robots at the time when we needed to test Lance. He was not designed to go up against combat robots and took many beatings from Lance being rammed, tipped, and flipped. Milo's old gears eventually gave out and Richard was finally assembled so Milo was retired to the shelf.

Design

Lance is a Tamiya TT-01 model from ancient times. He has 4 wheel drive and drives super smoothly. At least he used to. Now after spending too much time in the ring against Lance, his pinion gear is stripped and now he sounds like a dying goat when he tries to drive. He can barely move anymore but he succeeded in his job of being a training dummy for Lance.
