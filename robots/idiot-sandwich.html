Idiot Sandwich"The Flambe Fighter"

Description

Idiot Sandwich is a big mean rolling machine that was designed during the Summer '22 wave of 3lb projects. Emphasising passive evasion and a fire-based weapon, Idiot Sandwich was designed to be an aggressive bot that rushes down the opponent with a constant barrage of flames.

Design

Flame Roller uses big wheels to lift itself high up off the ground, positioning its main body out of reach of most weapons. I uses its "flamethrowers" (which are really specialized lighters) to damage the important components of opponents directly below. The intended strategy is thus to aim to stay on top of the opponent at all times, taking care to position its wheels out of harm's way. Its cylindrical form means it's tough to invert, and bars on its wheels mean it resists being flipped onto its wheels.

Trivia

  • Idiot Sandwich uses commercial lighters with a flame length of 8 inches.
  • Idiot Sandwich can self-right using its flipping bar.
  • Idiot Sandwich is made of plastic to save weight.
  • Idiot Sandwich loves to bake. It desperately wishes it could taste its creations.
