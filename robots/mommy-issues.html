Mommy Issues "Take him back, Rosie"

Builders

Aaron Harnish, Ethan Hernandez, Angela Zhu

Description

In the age of artificial intelligence, Daddy Issues was designed to crush opponents before the fight even begins. Armed with a roughishly handsome face and sculpted cheekbones, opponents swoon as soon as they see him in the arena and refuse to fight such a perfect being. His past trauma with his mother and (ex) girlfriend aside, Mommy Issues is the Ken of 3lb bots. He's a 10.

Design

On the impossible chance that Mommy Issues' opponent does not immediately surrender upon seeing his shiny exterior (perhaps the robot is blind), Mommy Issues has two aluminum arms that rotate vertically to attack the enemy, with a slanted end to lift oppponents. They're not puny, you are. Mommy Issues also has a unique walker drive, giving it a weight bonus and an alluring waddle.

Trivia

  • Mommy Issues calls his mom twice a day.
  • Mommy Issues has Rosie's name tattooed on its knuckles.
  • Mommy Issues owns an (authentic) Rolex that sprays cologne to attract other robots.
  • Mommy Issues has a relationship advice page in the Cornell Daily Sun.
