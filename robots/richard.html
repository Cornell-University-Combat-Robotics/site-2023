Richard "The Ripper"

Description

Richard, Kinetic’s first competition-ready robot, is a 12lb combat robot with a vertically spinning weapon. Richard has competed in 2 qualifying NHRL events and one championship event. Richard will forever be the favorite robot of the team. He is a vertical spinner robot whose weapon destroys the opponent with unmatched speed and power. The enemy that trembles before him gets smashed into the air, only to get crushed a second time upon impact with the ground. His aluminum armor protects him from most attacks.

Design

Richard was first designed in Fall 2021 and has had 3 other iterations since then. Richard’s chassis is fully made of Aluminum 6061 and its weapon was also originally this material. Once redesigned, Richard’s weapon material was AR400. Richard’s drivetrain has always been a bit of a challenge because the wheels are not aligned with the center of mass.
