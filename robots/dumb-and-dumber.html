Dumb and Dumber "The Dumb"

Builders

Blaze Ezlakowski, Charles Liu, Molly Drumm, Sebastian Rivera.

Description

These two idiots are chained together in an attempt to wrap around the enemy. Whether it works or not, they are going to be a blast to watch! The movie Dumb and Dumber was inspired by this robot. We met Jim Carrey himself and he was really inspired and felt compelled to direct and produce this hollywood classic. He gave us royalties for the movie and now CRC is very rich. We won't ever need funding anymore. Thank you Jimmy for your contributions to SCIENCE.

Design

The internals used are the same as Manny's internals (technically they are related). They were designed to be autonomous. Since they are a multi-bot, they get a weight bonus so they are actually 4.5 lbs despite competing in the 3 lb bracket. It is bulking season.

Trivia

  • The CAD is incomplete and uses parts from GrabCAD.
  • Dumb and Dumber has a wheel inside his triangle solid body.
  • Dumb can drive off Dumber's ramp so they can untangle themselves if necessary.
  • Dumb and Dumber is 4.5 lbs due to the multi-bot weight bonus.
  • If this robot was made in real life, it would not work at all.
