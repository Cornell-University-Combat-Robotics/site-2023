Biggie Cheese "The Boombastic"

Builders

Caleb Schlissel, Shao Stassen, Zander Knight.

Description

Born Chester Latorace Cheese, Biggie began rapping at the age of 9, and engaging in robot combat shortly after. They call him Mr. Boombastic, notoriously fantastic. Biggie Cheese is a talented musical artist, and a hopeless romantic. His rhymes break hearts, and his spinners shatter steel. There is no limit to his ubiquitous charm and appeal.

Design

The only thing that matches Biggie Cheese's beautiful chains are the bling of his weapons. His three interchangeable blades allow him to hit his opponent nearly as destructively as his incredible bars. With an aerodynamic curvy body, he can rizz up any opponent, and finish the job by flashing his bling in fabulous style. His phat wedge not only scoops up opponents, but also the W in any rap battle. Although he may be rather wide, his heart is even wider, but don't let his kindness betray you. He's never been defeated, whether on stage or in the cage.
