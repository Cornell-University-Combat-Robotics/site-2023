Florence "The Floppy Queen"

Description

Florence is a two-wheeled tri-flipper with a triangular chassis. Her weapon allows her to excel in defensive battle by allowing her to jump up into “table” positioning to dodge her opponents’ attacks. She can also preform the “Florence Flop” where she flails her arms up and down in an attempt to intimidate her enemies. Florence inspires fear in the heart of her enemies and turns their robot blood to ice when they see her Venus flytrap attack. In her last competition, Florence had the same win-loss ratio as Richard, only bowing out of the competition to allow her opponents the chance to feel loved by their parents (her circuit “didn’t work” or something).
