Brunobot & Lukebot "The Task Tracker"

Builders

Ethan Zhang, Grace Lim, Linda Hu, Luke Murphy, Sardor Rahmatulloev, Shao Stassen.

Description

Brunobot and Lukebot are the ghosts of our former team leads that guide current team leads with team management and task tracking. Sometimes, we can still hear their voices.

Design

Modeled after Team Lead Bruno Tassari and Vice Team Lead Luke Murphy in their ongoing effort to get CRC members to finish their tasks. It was designed using many grueling hours of looking at Slack API.

Trivia

  • The profile pictures of Brunobot and Lukebot are Sully and Mike Wazowski with their faces swapped.
  • Ironically, Brunobot and Lukebot was a task that was very much so procrastinated.
