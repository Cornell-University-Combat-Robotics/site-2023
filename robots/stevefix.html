Steve "The Star"

Builders

Ethan Zhang, James LaSalle, Sardor Rahmatulloev, Zarif Pathan.

Description

Steve once built a nice house made out of spruce. Steve likes to mine diamonds and impress his miner friends with his sharp diamond sword! Steve is CRC's grim reaper.

Design

Steve has Sharpness V on his pickaxes and pierces opponents from above with them. Once he has them trapped, he uses his front saw to do devastating damage. Almost as much damage as when his pet wolf showed up in the chat during a raid. Like this if you cry every time.

Trivia

  • Steve likes dirt.
  • Steve's favorite shape is a cube.
  • Steve loves making new friends with as many miners as possible.
  • Steve adores diamonds.
