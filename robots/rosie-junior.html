Rosie Junior "The Other Rizzler"

Builders

James Courtenay, James LaSalle, Isaac Newcomb, Mohammed Chowdhury, Spencer Hurst, Stella Taglich, Zander Knight.

Description

Rosie Junior was never actually in a fight. She waited patiently on the sidelines, hoping that L Billet (RIP) and Gorge Rusel would survive their dangerous ventures into the cage. Not because she was a coward, obviously, but because she just didn't want to hurt anyone if she had to fight.

Design

All Rosie Junior had to defend herself was one measly wedge, but luckily she never had to use it (once again not because she was a coward, she really just doesn't like to cause harm because she is such a nice bot). She, like L Billet and Gorge Rusel, was originally added to Rosie as an afterthought, and she still remains an afterthought since she was never actually in the ring. However, in Rosie Junior's own words, “it's better to be an afterthought than ripped to pieces like my friend L Billet.”

Trivia

  • You can't have any losses if you never fight at all.
  • World Record holder for fastest combat robot to ever be built.
  • Rosie Junior IS NOT a coward and she can't stress that enough. If anything, you're a coward! You don't know how it feels to not know if you're going to be thrown into a fight to the death in the next 20 minutes you idiot.
