Bobby & Bobbo "The Twins"

Builders

Unknown.

Description

These two have our hearts. They have a tendency to wander off every once in a while but they will be there at the end of the day. They actually one day showed up on the shelf. No one knows who and what placed them there. All we know is to worship them daily. Bobby is the larger one and is not the sharpest crayon in the box. Bobbo is the smaller one and is filled with unyielding rage.

Design

Made of vintage pink Legos. Bobby is designed to carry cargo and Bobbo is designed for maximum speed. Clearly, since they came from above, there is nothing to change because they are perfect.

Trivia

  • Bobbo sounds like an angry Italian man.
  • Bobby sounds like Patrick Star.
  • Bobby and Bobbo go to brunch on Sundays.
  • Bobby and Bobbo are roommates.
