CRC Chan "The Arcade"

Builders

Caleb Schlissel, Grace Lim, James LaSalle, Linda Hu, Luke Murphy.

Description

CRC Chan is our team's arcade machine that currently houses CRC VS. She is a magical girl that wanders the ELL at night. If you say her name 3 times in front of the Upson basement bathrooms during witching hour, she will show up and grant you a wish... with a twist... you won't remember that it ever happened.

Design

The Arcade's color scheme is based on our team's colors, black and red. On the back, CRC Chan is painted in white and is based on Logobot, CRC's logo. She bears a scythe now that she has transformed into her magical girl form.

Trivia

  • Logobot is CRC Chan's pet.
  • CRC Kun is out to get milk.
  • CRC Chan appears in your dreams once you become an official CRC member.
