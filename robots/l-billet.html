L Billet "The Little B"

Builders

James Courtenay, James LaSalle, Isaac Newcomb, Mohammed Chowdhury, Spencer Hurst, Stella Taglich, Zander Knight.

Description

The L Billet is probably the best-known mini bot on CRC. His heroic sacrifice during his one and only match single handedly broke the enemy bot's weapon. Sadly the match was still lost but L Billet will never be forgotten. L Billet has a built in turbo button on his controller which allows him to hit incredible speeds.

Design

The L Billet is armed with nothing but a wedge and his bravery. He was originally added to Rosie as an afterthought but soon became the star of the duo. L Billet used Richards' old wheel guards as a wedge which allowed him to distract opponents. He also used one of Richard's old side plates as a spoiler for extra speed.
