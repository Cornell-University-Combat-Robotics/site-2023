Ladies and gentlemen, behold the unrivaled mechanical marvel known as Black Beetle! Design

Black Beetle was designed with the main focus on defense and control rather than damage. He was built around a pneumatic flipper that could flip opponents around the arena and hold them in the air.

Trivia
