Description

Stuffed Manny is the stuffed animal incarnation of Manny. Manny was once CRC's mascot and every mascot needs merchandise.

Design

Made of soft fibers and crafted with sewing prowess, Stuffed Manny is a plump model. His wheel often falls off, but there is a dedicated manicure session that Stuffed Manny goes to fit it.

Trivia

  • Stuffed Manny's weapon is backwards.
  • Stuffed Manny does not like being sat on.
